Hydration

Routine Meals

Establish regular meal and snack times to provide structure and ensure consistent energy levels throughout the day.

Better Sleep Habits üåô

Sleep is crucial for a child's brain development and physical growth. Establishing a calming bedtime routine is key.

  • Stick to a schedule: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Create a calm environment: Keep the bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Limit screens: Avoid electronic devices at least an hour before bedtime.
  • Relaxing routine: Read a book or take a warm bath before sleep.

Creating a safe environment allows your child to explore the world with confidence.

  • Childproofing: Secure furniture, cover outlets, and keep hazardous substances out of reach.
  • Water Safety: Never leave children unattended near water, including bathtubs.
  • Car Safety: Always use an age-appropriate car seat or booster seat properly installed in the back seat.
